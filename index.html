ABOUT US

ARAK is a multidisciplinary infrastructure engineering and project management consulting firm offering a wide
range of consulting and advisory services and providing expertise in engineering, design and construction. Our
comprehensive services include planning and advisory services and providing expertise in engineering, design
and construction. Our comprehensive services include planning and consulting; Transport Engineering; Project
and Construction Management; Urban development, Architecture; Energy; Water and Environmental management.

Our professionals have a broad range of backgrounds and experience, consequently, our designs incorporate co-
ordination of all disciplines as an inherent part of the design philosophy. By constantly working in multi-disciplinary
engineers gain greater appreciation of other disciplines encouraging them to look beyond the constraints of their specialties.

Our collaborative and dynamic approach not only strives to deliver highly innovative and buildable designs by
applying contemporary techniques but also developing new solutions for tomorrow. A key element in our
approach is direct involvement of the directors and associate directors in the firm’s projects. Each project has a
responsible director who is accountable for the successful completion of the work. In order to meet our client
expectations we are constantly open to collaborations with local and international firms to ensure project
objectives are meet by bringing our expertise and commitment into these collaborations.
